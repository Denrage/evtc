Enum CoreSpecialization

A trait specialization that is not a EliteSpecialization.

Namespace: GW2Scratch.EVTCAnalytics.Model
Assembly: GW2Scratch.EVTCAnalytics.dll
Syntax
public enum CoreSpecialization
Remarks

Each player may have up to 3 CoreSpecialization accessible to their Profession at a time, and one of them may be an EliteSpecialization instead.

Each CoreSpecialization is only available to one Profession as of the Path of Fire expansion.
